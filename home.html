Let us discover and try to improve your Reading Comprehension Level

Comprehension is the understanding and interpretation of what is read. To be able to accurately understand written material, people need to be able to (1) decode what they read; (2) make connections between what they read and what they already know; and (3) think deeply about what they have read.

That is why, this website will help you determine the level of your reading comprehension and help to improve it by the use of effective strategies.
